# Search And PRISMA Protocol

Use this reference when designing database searches, inclusion criteria, and reproducible screening records.

## 1. Database Strategy

Default for bibliometric manuscripts:

- Web of Science Core Collection;
- Scopus.

Add based on question:

- PubMed when health outcomes, epidemiology, or exposure-response evidence is central;
- SPORTDiscus when sport science and physical activity facilities are central;
- Dimensions/OpenAlex only as supplementary discovery or citation checking, not as the sole final database unless justified.

## 2. Search Blocks

Build search strings from separate blocks.

### Object Block

Use for active recreation and green/open spaces:

`sport* facilit* OR sports facilit* OR sport* park* OR sports park* OR recreation* facilit* OR physical activity resource* OR fitness facilit* OR playground* OR urban park* OR public open space* OR green space* OR green infrastructure OR pocket park*`

### Measurement Block

`accessib* OR exposure OR availab* OR proximity OR service area* OR catchment OR walkability OR travel time OR network distance OR 2SFCA OR floating catchment OR gravity OR supply-demand OR location-allocation OR park use OR visitation`

### Equity And Justice Block

`equit* OR inequal* OR disparit* OR deprivation OR underserved OR vulnerable OR environmental justice OR spatial justice OR distributional justice OR procedural justice OR recognition justice OR social vulnerability`

### Health And Activity Block

`physical activity OR active living OR walking OR exercise OR wellbeing OR mental health OR obesity OR cardiovascular OR mortality OR urban health OR healthy city`

### Heat And Climate Block

`heat exposure OR thermal comfort OR urban heat OR cooling service* OR heatwave* OR climate adaptation OR shade OR temperature`

### Exclusion Block

Exclude when needed:

`elite sport* OR professional sport* OR sport event* OR mega-event* OR sport tourism OR sports medicine OR injury OR rehabilitation OR physical education OR coaching OR curriculum`

## 3. Search Documentation

Record:

- database;
- platform;
- search date;
- full query;
- years;
- document types;
- language;
- indexes;
- initial hits;
- records after filters;
- records after de-duplication;
- title/abstract exclusions;
- full-text exclusions with reasons;
- final analytical set.

Use `assets/PRISMA筛选记录模板.xlsx`.

## 4. Inclusion Criteria

Include studies that:

- examine urban sport, green, park, playground, recreation, open-space, or physical-activity resources;
- measure accessibility, exposure, availability, proximity, use, quality, cooling access, or supply-demand mismatch;
- address equity, justice, inequality, vulnerability, or differential health/activity opportunity;
- have clear urban, neighbourhood, regional, or spatial planning relevance;
- are in Q1/Q2 or target high-quality journals when building a training corpus.

## 5. Exclusion Criteria

Exclude studies dominated by:

- elite/professional sport performance;
- sport events, tourism, branding, sponsorship, broadcast media;
- sports injury, rehabilitation, clinical exercise training;
- physical education pedagogy and school curriculum unless playground/schoolyard spatial equity is the focus;
- purely ecological green-space studies without accessibility, exposure, equity, or health/activity relevance;
- purely technical remote-sensing papers without planning or exposure implication.

## 6. Screening Rule

Screen in this order:

1. title;
2. abstract;
3. full text;
4. method relevance;
5. journal/partition verification;
6. final evidence role.

Do not remove a paper only because it is not "sports"; remove it only if it cannot support the concept chain.
