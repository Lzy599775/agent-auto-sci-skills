Auto-sci-research: a Codex-first skill suite for full research writing and analysis.

这个项目服务于体育公园、体育设施、绿地暴露、热暴露、空间公平和城市健康研究。它不是自动“写论文”的捷径，而是一套能积累证据、固化流程、提醒风险并支持持续进化的科研工作台。

This project supports research on sport parks, sport facilities, green exposure, heat exposure, spatial equity, and urban health. It is a maintainable research workbench, not a shortcut paper generator.

每个 skill 做什么

What Each Skill Does

下面是面向使用者的详细解释。真正的可执行说明在各自的 SKILL.md 和 references/ 中。

Below is a user-facing overview. The executable instructions live in each SKILL.md and its references/.

auto-sci-research

总控 skill，适合跨多个研究阶段的任务。它先判断任务属于文献、方法、数据、空间、模型、写作还是 skill 进化，再调用最合适的子 skill。

Umbrella router for multi-stage research tasks. It identifies whether the task is about literature, methods, data, spatial analysis, ML, writing, or skill evolution.

  • 维护研究链路和长期记忆
  • 避免重复创建子 skill
  • 要求重大变动写入进化档案
  • Maintains research routes and reusable memory
  • Avoids duplicate subskills
  • Requires evolution records for major changes

agent-auto-sci-automation

把自动科研项目中有价值的部分转成可控流程，例如 source manifest、checkpoint、失败经验、任务恢复和 API 安全。

Turns useful automation ideas into controlled workflows: source manifests, checkpoints, failure logs, task recovery, and API safety.

  • 适合长期项目管理
  • 强调密钥和私有数据边界
  • 记录失败和复盘，而不是只记录成功
  • Designed for long-running projects
  • Defines boundaries for secrets and private data
  • Records failures and lessons learned

agent-auto-sci-methodology

用于把模糊选题变成可检验的研究问题，整理机制路径、假设矩阵、证据强度和偏倚风险。

Turns vague topics into testable research questions, mechanism pathways, hypothesis matrices, evidence strength, and bias audits.

  • 区分相关、机制和因果主张
  • 检查空间公平与环境正义框架
  • 帮助写出更稳的局限和政策边界
  • Separates association, mechanism, and causal claims
  • Checks spatial equity and justice frameworks
  • Improves limitations and policy boundaries

agent-auto-sci-geospatial

服务于 GIS、遥感和暴露评估任务，关注 CRS、网络可达性、NDVI/LST、LCZ、地图合规和空间偏差。

Supports GIS, remote sensing, and exposure assessment, including CRS, network accessibility, NDVI/LST, LCZ, map compliance, and spatial bias.

  • 体育设施与公园可达性
  • 绿色暴露与热暴露
  • 空间公平、尺度效应和 MAUP 风险
  • Sport facility and park accessibility
  • Green and heat exposure
  • Spatial equity, scale effects, and MAUP risks

agent-auto-sci-data-viz

把数据分析和论文图表放在同一套逻辑里：先审计数据，再做 EDA、统计和图件，最后回到论文 claim。

Connects data analysis and manuscript figures: audit data, run EDA/statistics, build visuals, then map visuals back to claims.

  • 适合文献计量图、政策矩阵和空间图表
  • 强调 caption 支撑主张
  • 避免只做“好看但没论点”的图
  • Useful for bibliometric visuals, policy matrices, and spatial figures
  • Captions must support claims
  • Avoids visuals without argument value

agent-auto-sci-ai-ml

适合地理学与体育学项目中的机器学习建模。重点不是堆模型，而是 baseline、泄露检查、空间验证和解释边界。

For ML in geography and sport-science projects. The focus is not model stacking, but baselines, leakage checks, spatial validation, and interpretation boundaries.

  • SHAP/XAI 不能直接写成因果解释
  • 空间/时间切分优先于随机切分
  • 模型性能必须配合可解释性和稳健性
  • SHAP/XAI is not causal evidence by default
  • Spatial/temporal splits are preferred over random splits
  • Performance must be paired with interpretability and robustness

agent-auto-sci-scicomm

负责科学写作和交流，包括论文结构、图文叙事、投稿信、审稿回复、PPT 和海报。

Handles scientific writing and communication: manuscript structure, figure-story alignment, cover letters, rebuttals, slides, and posters.

  • 使用 claim-evidence map 检查论证
  • 适配目标期刊的问题意识
  • 把审稿风险前置处理
  • Uses claim-evidence maps
  • Adapts arguments to target journals
  • Surfaces reviewer risks early

urban-exposure-review-radar-workflow

新增的综述雷达全流程 skill。它先判断任务应走系统综述、范围综述、文献计量+批判性综述、叙事/批判性综述、前沿雷达还是实证设计，再把检索、筛选、提取、编码、图表、写作和目标期刊门控串起来。

The new review-radar workflow skill. It first decides whether the task is a systematic review, scoping review, bibliometric + critical review, narrative/critical review, frontier radar, or empirical design, then connects search, screening, extraction, coding, figures, writing, and journal gates.

  • 区分 formal corpus 与 arXiv/GitHub/会议页面等 radar candidates
  • 为 Cities/SCS/EI/Health & Place/UFUG/Nature-family 设置证据门槛
  • 覆盖绿色暴露、热暴露、体育设施可达性、遥感热环境、Geospatial AI 和医学数据库连接
  • Separates formal corpora from arXiv/GitHub/conference radar candidates
  • Adds journal evidence gates for Cities, SCS, EI, Health & Place, UFUG, and Nature-family outlets
  • Covers green exposure, heat exposure, sport facility accessibility, remote-sensing heat, Geospatial AI, and health database linkage

sport-geography-review-bibliometric

体育地理综述与文献计量专用 skill。路线可以是系统综述、scoping review、文献计量，也可以是“综述 + 批判性框架 + 政策议程”。

A sport-geography skill for systematic reviews, scoping reviews, bibliometrics, or review + critical framework + policy agenda manuscripts.

  • PRISMA 与检索式设计
  • CiteSpace / VOSviewer / R bibliometrix 图表规划
  • 批判性编码和政策议程
  • PRISMA and search strategy
  • CiteSpace / VOSviewer / R bibliometrix figure planning
  • Critical coding and policy agenda

sport-geography-sci-writing

体育地理实证论文写作 skill。适合体育设施可达性、体育公园暴露、绿地/热暴露、公平与健康行为机制研究。

Empirical SCI writing skill for sport geography, facility accessibility, sport park exposure, green/heat exposure, equity, and health behavior mechanisms.

  • 目标期刊定位和叙事路线
  • 方法、结果、讨论和政策启示
  • 图表、公式和补充材料组织
  • Target-journal positioning and narrative route
  • Methods, results, discussion, and policy implications
  • Figures, formulas, and supplementary materials
