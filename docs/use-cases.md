## Policy Agenda

A strong policy agenda links:

```text
evidence gap -> affected population -> spatial mechanism -> intervention lever -> data requirement -> implementation risk
```

## Pre-submission Audit

Use before submission:

```text
agent-auto-sci-scicomm
-> agent-auto-sci-methodology
-> sport-geography-sci-writing
```

Check:

- every claim has evidence;
- maps and figures support necessary claims;
- causal language matches research design;
- policy recommendations are specific and evidence-bounded;
- limitations are honest and not self-defeating.
